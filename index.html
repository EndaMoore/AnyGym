AnyGym A pay as you use service that allows entrance to any participating gym in Ireland.

Why Choose Us?

Here are just a few reasons to choose AnyGym for all of your Fitness requirements


The Situation

Sedentary lives + Convenient food solutions = Rise in obesity

With overcrowded gyms, fixed membership contracts and belonging to only one gym

It’s no wonder that 30-50% of members leave a gym within 6 months of joining.

The Solution

We’re putting experience, value and flexibility back into the Health & Fitness Industry.

We’re giving our members live capacity of each gym so they can choose their preferred atmosphere,

We’re providing them an option to pay per hour, only for what they use based on the amount of people inside at the time

And we’re allowing instant access and a Universal Membership
